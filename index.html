What

are Flashcards?

Flashcards — small two-sided cards with prompt on one side and the information about the prompt on the other — is a self-testing approach. In fact, flashcards are hands-down the most effective way for motivated learners to study and retain factual knowledge. Some research has found that this kind of active recall retrieval practice leads to 150% better retention than passive studying. With our website, you can take this method to the next level!

Why

Damuword?

Damuword is an online platform where students and teachers can create sets of flashcards, and print them for free! Moreover, you can share sets with your friends or you can study with ready flashcards. Damuword gives you an opportunity to have neat flashcards and save your time!
